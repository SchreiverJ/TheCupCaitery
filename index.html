Meet Caite

Caite graduated from Sullivan University Culinary Arts Program with an Associate of Science degree in Baking & Pastry Arts. She is a Certified Pastry Chef by the American Culinary Federation and is ServSafe Certified.

She loves to share her passion for baking in her spare time, while being a stay at home mom for two beautiful little girls. In her spare time she also likes to paint and do DIY projects around the house.

Before her daughters were born, Caite worked at several bakeries in the Louisville and Northern Kentucky area. At Annie May's Sweet Cafe in Louisville, KY, she mastered the practice of allergen-friendly baking. At Fantasy in Frosting in Newport, she continued to improve her award-winning design skills.

Caite produces baked goods under the Kentucky Home Baker laws. Learn more about Kentucky Home Bakers
