## Pricing Strategy Notes

### Our Position
- **Affordable specialty** - not bargain, not premium
- Competitive with local bakeries
- Below national chain prices (Crumbl)
- Premium justified by: ACF certified pastry chef, custom work, quality ingredients

### Competitive Advantages
1. True customization (logos, themes, personalization)
2. Custom 3D-printed cookie cutters
3. Professional credentials (Sullivan culinary degree, ACF certified, ServSafe)
4. Local/personal service under Kentucky Home Baker laws

### Price Increase Considerations
- Ingredient costs
- Design complexity
- Rush orders (consider 30% rush fee like other bakeries)
- Delivery (if offered)
- Large order discounts
